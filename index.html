Sitio Bebe Fundraiser Concert Plan

Our chosen category for promoting the fundraiser is Video Blog.


The reason for this being that with Video Blog, we can further promote the fundraiser concert through videos uploaded online. That way, people around the world can be notified about the fundraiser. It will also help to make people online aware of the situation happening in Sitio Bebe and how they can help by attending the fundraiser concert. People who wish to attend the concert but can't due to the pandemic will be able to watch the process of planning the concert, the performance of Ben&Ben, and the visit to Sitio Bebe for the distribution of the funds that came from the concert.

We have four kinds of audiences that we plan on attracting which are the following:



Philanthropists

Patrons

Music Enjoyers

Ben&Ben fans



Ben&Ben fans wish to stay updated on Ben&Ben's activities such as live concerts. And with the video blog, they can watch their performance even if they can't make it. Music enjoyers wish love musical entertainment so of course they'd want to check out the concert. And as for Patrons and Philanthropists, they'd want to check out the concert's details and info to really know what they'll be supporting if they ever decide to help out the fundraiser.
